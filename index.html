Lots-o'-Huggin' Bear

Lots-o'-Huggin' Bear , or "Lotso" for short, is the main antagonist from the movie Toy Story 3. He is a large, pink teddy bear that smells like strawberries.
He was a beloved toy until his owner accidentally dropped him and left him. When Lotso came back to the owner, he saw that he was replaced by a different toy and became evil.

  • Although he is a villain in the movie, he has his own history and reason why he became evil.
